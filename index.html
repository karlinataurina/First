Christmas Dessert
Ingredient list:
Mandarins
Raspberries
Brown sugar
Coconut milk
Dark chocolate
Vegan gingerbread
Recipe:
Refrigerate can of coconut milk, 8 hours or overnight. Open can of coconut milk, taking care not to shake it. Scoop coconut cream solids into a mixing bowl. Reserve remaining liquid for another use. Beat coconut cream and brown sugar using electric mixer with chilled beaters on medium speed; turn to high speed. Beat until stiff peaks form, 7 to 8 minutes. Break gingerbread cookies into small pieces, cut mandarins into smaller pieces. Add the gingerbread cookies, mandarins and raspberries to coconut cream, mix and leave for gingerbread to soak up the cream and become softer. Serve into a glass with some grated dark chocolate and berries on top!
